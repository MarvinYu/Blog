Text

Verse 1
One night I was a monkey
I was sitting on a table
but I couldn't move at all
for I was tied up with a cable,
then I panicked and I realized
my fighting was in vain,
there were fifteen human animals
longing for my brain.

Verse 2
One day was like each other
and my cage was dark and tiny
all I ate was coke 'n chocolate
got my teeth cleaned bright and shiny.
I was far too fat to stand
got almost strangled by my chain
and the image of the forest
vanished from my brain.

Verse 3
I saw a monkey's mother
with her young one always with her
and the young one must have heard
all the whips that they still give her.
She was all the world to him
but all he could feel was pain.
So they opened up his head
to eat his lovely brain.
