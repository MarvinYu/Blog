Resident at Recurse Center

A talk at Recurse Center, New York, NY, USA, 28 Sep 2015

This is not a conference talk (I just don’t have time to customise my blog right now, so I’m listing it as a talk for lack of a better category).

As a resident, I will be spending two weeks full-time at the Recurse Center, a community and educational retreat for people who want to get better at programming. They are an excellent group of people, and I am lucky to get to spend two weeks with them.
